How the Platform Works

A streamlined process connecting institutes, interviewers, and students in one structured platform.

1

Institute Registers

Institutes submit a request specifying departments, student count, and preferred interview domains and window.

2

Admin Assigns Interviewers

The admin panel reviews requests, assigns domain-matched expert interviewers and schedules sessions.

3

Live Interview Session

Interviewers conduct structured sessions with each student, taking notes and reviewing their resume in real-time.

4

Structured Evaluation

After each session, interviewers complete a detailed rubric covering technical, communication and domain skills.

5

Reports & Insights

Institutes receive comprehensive student performance reports and interviewers track their session history.
